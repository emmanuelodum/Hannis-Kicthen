Peace grew up destined to be part of the food industry in Nigeria.

She always had immense love for food and has always been very fond of cooking since her childhood. Therefore she automatically decided to make a career out of her passion while also creating job opportunities within her locallity since 2014.

While growing up, Peace enjoyed working alongside seasoned food industry professionals as they created delicious food masterpieces for locals and multinationals. She knew then that she wanted a career preparing mouthwatering cuisine just like her mentors once she reached adulthood.

Under her watch, Hannis kitchen has transformed into a global viable food company of high repute within and beyond the shores of Nigeria.
